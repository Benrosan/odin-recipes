Bruschetta Recipe

Classic Italian dish. I like!.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, cut into 1/4 inch slices
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly-ground black pepper

Recipe

  1. Toast Bread 10 minutes at 400 degrees in oven.
  2. Mince ingredient: tomatoes, garlic, basil and parmesan.
  3. Mix together: Toss with olive oil and vinegar and spoon onto bread.
