Boeuf Bourguignon Recipe

The pot roast's progenitor but French, so better.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 5 onions, sliced
  • 2 lbs of lean beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • Freshly-ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 8 ounces of fresh-cut mushrooms

Recipe

  1. Heat the oil: cook and stir onions until tender.
  2. Cook beef: until browned. Sprinkle in thyme, poor wine and reduce over low simmer
  3. Stir onions and mushrooms: Cook, stirring occasionally until mushrooms are tender and sauce is thick and dark brown.
